================================================================================
                    BEELINK GTR9 PRO - FULL SPECIFICATION SHEET
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
The Beelink GTR9 Pro is a compact, all-metal mini workstation powered by the
AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 APU. At ~$2,000, it packs workstation-class AI compute
into a chassis smaller than a shoebox. It is the foundation of a solo-dev AI
R&D lab.


================================================================================
PROCESSOR: AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395
================================================================================

  Architecture .......... AMD Zen 5 (4nm TSMC)
  Cores / Threads ....... 16 / 32
  Base Clock ............ 3.0 GHz
  Boost Clock ........... 5.1 GHz
  L3 Cache .............. 64 MB
  Socket ................ FP11
  TDP (configured) ...... 140W


================================================================================
AI COMPUTE
================================================================================

  NPU ................... AMD XDNA 2
  NPU TOPS .............. 50 TOPS (INT8)
  Total Platform TOPS ... 126 TOPS (CPU + GPU + NPU combined)


================================================================================
INTEGRATED GPU: AMD Radeon 8060S
================================================================================

  Architecture .......... RDNA 3.5
  Compute Units ......... 40
  Stream Processors ..... 2,560
  Base Clock ............ 1,295 MHz
  Boost Clock ........... 2,335 MHz
  FP32 Performance ...... ~12-15 TFLOPS (single-issue) / ~29.7 TFLOPS (dual)
  Max Assignable VRAM ... 96 GB (Windows) / ~110 GB (Linux)
  L2 Cache .............. 8 MB
  API Support ........... DirectX 12 Ultimate (12_2), Vulkan 1.3

  GPU PERFORMANCE CONTEXT:
  - Sits between RTX 4060 and RTX 4070 Laptop in gaming/graphics benchmarks
  - Outperforms RTX 4090 by ~220% in AI efficiency (tokens/watt)
  - Can run models up to 128B parameters that won't fit in any consumer GPU


================================================================================
MEMORY
================================================================================

  Type .................. LPDDR5X
  Speed ................. 8000 MHz
  Capacity .............. 128 GB
  Bandwidth ............. ~256 GB/s
  Soldered .............. Yes (not upgradeable)

  WHY THIS MEMORY MATTERS FOR AI:
  The 128 GB unified memory pool is the single biggest differentiator. Consumer
  GPUs top out at 24 GB (RTX 4090) or 48 GB (RTX 5090). The Beelink can assign
  up to 96-110 GB to the GPU, allowing it to load massive models entirely into
  GPU-accessible memory that would otherwise require $10,000+ enterprise hardware.


================================================================================
STORAGE
================================================================================

  Included .............. 2 TB Crucial NVMe SSD
  Slots ................. Dual M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0
  Max Capacity .......... 8 TB (2x 4TB)
  Max Speed ............. ~7,000 MB/s


================================================================================
NETWORKING
================================================================================

  Wired ................. Dual Intel 10 GbE E610-XT2 (2x 10Gbps)
  WiFi .................. WiFi 7
  Bluetooth ............. 5.4

  !! KNOWN ISSUE !!
  The Intel E610-XT2 NICs have documented instability under sustained mixed
  GPU + network workloads. This was escalated to Intel and Beelink as of
  October 2025. Monitor firmware updates.

================================================================================
LLM INFERENCE BENCHMARKS (Community-Verified)
================================================================================

  Small models (7B-8B) ....... 30-50+ TPS       (With ROCm on Linux)
  Qwen3:32b .................. ~15 TPS           (Ollama, without ROCm)
  GPT-OSS:20b ................ ~50 TPS           (ROCm, prompt eval 500+ TPS)
  70B dense models ........... ~3-5 TPS          (Fits in memory; batch OK)
  128B+ models ............... Runs              (Llama 4 Scout 109B confirmed)

  KEY ADVANTAGE:
  This machine can load and run 70B-128B parameter models that physically
  cannot fit into the VRAM of an RTX 4090 (24 GB), RTX 5090, or even a
  Mac M4 Pro. Only enterprise GPUs (A100 80GB, H100) or multi-GPU setups
  can match this capability, at 5-20x the cost.

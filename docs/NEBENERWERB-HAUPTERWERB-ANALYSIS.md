## 1. Health Insurance (Krankenkasse) — The Big Question

### The Fear
The standard rule for self-employed individuals in Germany: you MUST have German health insurance. If Haupterwerb triggers this, you'd be looking at €300–800+/month for private health insurance, or income-dependent contributions for public insurance. This alone could make the switch financially irrational.

### The Reality for SOFA-Status Individuals
A **Federal Social Court (Bundessozialgericht) ruling of 20.03.2013** addressed TRICARE specifically. The court found that:

- TRICARE **does not technically meet** the German Versicherungspflicht (insurance obligation) under §193 VVG
- **However**, TRICARE IS recognized as "anderweitige Absicherung" (alternative coverage) that **excludes you from the obligation**

In practice: TRICARE creates a carve-out. You are not forced to obtain German health insurance because you already have recognized alternative coverage. This applies regardless of Haupt-/Nebenerwerb classification.

### ⚠️ Caveat
This is based on a 2013 ruling and general legal interpretation. Your specific situation (SOFA dependent, UG Geschäftsführer, American citizen) should be confirmed with:
1. Your **Steuerberater** (who should know SOFA tax/insurance specifics)
2. The **garrison legal office (JAG)** at Grafenwöhr/Vilseck
3. Optionally, a brief inquiry to your **Krankenkasse contact** (if you have one from ELSTER setup)

**Bottom line:** Highly likely that TRICARE continues to cover you. But confirm before filing.

## 2. Pension Insurance (Rentenversicherung) — No Obligation

### As Sole Gesellschafter-Geschäftsführer
You hold **100% of shares** in ML Upskill Agents UG. Per established case law ([IHK Köln guidance](https://www.ihk.de/koeln/hauptnavigation/recht-steuern/sozialversicherungspflicht-des-gmbh-gf-merkblatt--5155806)), a Gesellschafter-Geschäftsführer with ≥50% shares has "maßgeblicher Einfluss" (decisive influence) over the company. This means:

- You are **NOT considered a dependent employee** (kein abhängig Beschäftigter)
- Standard employee social insurance (Kranken-, Pflege-, Renten-, Arbeitslosenversicherung) does **NOT apply** through the company
- You handle your own insurance as a self-employed person

### As a Self-Employed Person
Mandatory pension insurance for self-employed people in Germany only applies to **specific professions**: craftspeople (Handwerker), teachers, midwives, artists/journalists (via Künstlersozialkasse), and a few others. Software developers and data scientists are **NOT** in the mandatory categories.

The one exception: **Scheinselbständigkeit** (pseudo-self-employment) — if you work for essentially one client providing >5/6 of revenue. With multiple micro-SaaS products and diverse client base, this doesn't apply.

**Bottom line:** Switching to Haupterwerb does NOT create a pension insurance obligation.

## 3. German Income Tax — Already Applies, No Change

Per NATO SOFA Article X paragraph 2 and Article 68 paragraph 4 of the Supplementary Agreement:

> Self-employment income generated within the German economy is subject to German taxes, regardless of SOFA status.

You already have a Steuernummer (201/132/50136) and file Körperschaftsteuer and Umsatzsteuer. The Nebenerwerb → Haupterwerb switch changes **nothing** about your tax obligations. The company pays Körperschaftsteuer (~15% + Solidaritätszuschlag) and Gewerbesteuer regardless. Your personal income from the UG (Geschäftsführergehalt) is subject to German Einkommensteuer either way.

**Bottom line:** No tax change.

## 4. IHK (Industrie- und Handelskammer) Membership — Already Applies

As a UG registered in Amberg (HRB 8114), you're already an IHK member. IHK membership fees are based on revenue/profit. The Haupt-/Nebenerwerb distinction does NOT affect IHK fees — they look at the company's financials regardless.

At pre-revenue with €1 Stammkapital, you're likely paying the minimum fee (often €0 for the first 1–2 years for new Existenzgründer, then ~€50–150/year minimum).

**Bottom line:** No meaningful change.

## 5. Unemployment Insurance (Arbeitslosenversicherung) — No Obligation

As a self-employed Geschäftsführer with 100% shares, you are NOT subject to mandatory unemployment insurance. There is a voluntary option ("freiwillige Weiterversicherung") through the Agentur für Arbeit, but this is entirely optional and unrelated to Haupt-/Nebenerwerb status.

**Bottom line:** No change.

## 6. SOFA-Specific Considerations

### Can SOFA Dependents Operate a Haupterwerb?
Yes. The [Stuttgart garrison guide](https://home.army.mil/stuttgart/application/files/9615/4712/5688/SEG.pdf) and [Ramstein SOFA guide](https://www.ramstein.af.mil/Portals/6/SOFA%20STATUS%20-%20WHAT%20DOES%20THIS%20MEAN%20TO%20YOU.pdf) confirm that SOFA dependents CAN be self-employed in Germany. The key restrictions are:

1. You **cannot use SOFA privileges for your business** (e.g., tax-free AAFES shopping for business supplies, APO for business mail)
2. You are **fully subject to German tax and social security law** for business income
3. Your SOFA status itself is **not affected** by having a Haupterwerb

### Does Haupterwerb Affect Your SOFA Status?
No. SOFA status derives from your spouse's (India's) DoDEA employment. Your personal employment or business activity doesn't change it. You remain a SOFA dependent regardless.

### Does It Affect Your Spouse's DoDEA Employment?
No. Your business classification has no bearing on India's employment status.

## 7. The Actual Gewerbeummeldung Process

### What to File
A **Gewerbeummeldung** (business re-registration) at the Gewerbeamt Vilseck. This changes the business classification from Nebenerwerb to Haupterwerb without needing a new Gewerbeanmeldung.

### Where
Gewerbeamt Vilseck (same office where you filed the original Gewerbe-Anmeldung, Aktenzeichen: gew - 34 - 2025 - Gr)

### Cost
Typically €20–40 (varies by municipality)

### What Changes on the Form
- Gewerbe Type: ~~Nebenerwerb~~ → **Haupterwerb**
- Business activities, address, and everything else stays the same

### Automatic Notifications
After filing, the Gewerbeamt automatically notifies:
- Finanzamt Amberg (no action needed from you)
- IHK (no action needed)
- Statistisches Landesamt (no action needed)

### Timeline
Same-day or next-day processing at small Gewerbeämter like Vilseck.

## Programs Eliminated — Why

### AI NATION GRANT ❌ (Remove from Tracker)
Two disqualifiers:
1. **Requires minimum 2 committed founders.** You are a solo founder.
2. **2026 application window (Jan 2 – Feb 1) already closed.** Pitch days were March 10–12.

Even for future cycles, the 2-founder requirement is structural. Unless you bring on a co-founder, this is permanently ineligible.

### EXIST-Gründerstipendium ❌ (Remove from Tracker)
The disqualifier is the **legal form**, not company age or revenue. EXIST treats Kapitalgesellschaften (GmbH, UG, etc.) differently from sole proprietorships:

- **Gewerbeanmeldung / Einzelunternehmen**: A mere business registration does NOT automatically disqualify — eligibility depends on whether the project is "operatively active on the market." Pre-revenue freelancers can potentially still qualify.
- **Kapitalgesellschaft (UG, GmbH)**: Hard stop. Per the directive: *"Die Förderung bereits gegründeter Kapitalgesellschaften (GmbH, UG etc.) über das EXIST-Gründerstipendium ist durch die Richtlinie untersagt."* ("Funding of already-founded capital companies is prohibited by the directive.")

Since ML Upskill Agents is a **UG (haftungsbeschränkt)** — a Kapitalgesellschaft — this is a structural disqualifier. Pre-revenue status does not help. Definitively ineligible.

Source: [Existenzgründungsportal — UG während des Studiums gegründet: EXIST?](https://www.existenzgruendungsportal.de/Redaktion/DE/BMWK-Infopool/Antworten/Foerderung-Finanzierung/Foerderung/EXIST-Gruenderstipendium/UG-Unternehmergesellschaft-waehrend-des-Studiums-gegruendet-EXIST)

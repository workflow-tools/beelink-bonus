## Notes for Steuerberater

1. **Monthly VAT returns** are required (Kalendermonat), not quarterly. Consider applying for Dauerfristverlängerung if monthly filing is burdensome initially.

2. **Nebenerwerb classification** — The Gewerbe was registered as a side business. This may affect Gewerbesteuer treatment and should be discussed with the Steuerberater as revenue grows. At what point should this be reclassified as Haupterwerb?

3. **SOFA-Stamp as Aufenthaltstitel** — The Gewerbe-Anmeldung explicitly records the SOFA stamp as the basis for residence. This confirms the Stadt accepted SOFA status for trade registration, but does not address the income tax implications of private business income under SOFA (see `01-SOFA-STATUS-AND-PERSONAL-TAX.md`).

4. **Stammkapital of €1** — The absolute legal minimum. The 25% annual profit retention obligation applies until this reaches €25,000. See `02-GERMAN-CORPORATE-TAX.md`.

5. **Gegenstand is broad** — "Datengestützte Studien- und Karriereberatung" plus "Beratungen in Datenanalyse, KI, und Instructional Design" covers all planned SaaS products without needing a Gegenstandsänderung (purpose amendment). No action needed here.
